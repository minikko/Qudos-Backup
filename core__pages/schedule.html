How to schedule rides with QudosSchedule

After setting your destination, a pop up menu will be displayed. Tap the QudosSchedule icon to proceed.

Set the date and time for your scheduled pickup Tap Set to proceed. Select batch or a specific driver from your QudosFave.

How to schedule rides with QudosSchedule

Tap the Qudos Menu icon, then tap on Scheduled Rides. Scroll through the dates to view your scheduled rides.
