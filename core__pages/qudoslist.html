What can you do with QudosList™

If you sign up for Qudos, you automatically have a QudosList account. You can now connect to a community of riders and drivers.

Post.

Learn how to post on QudosList and become a part of a ride-hailing community.

Share.

Learn how to share your favorite driver on QudosList.

Know more.

Learn how to know more about your driver and add them on your QudosFave.

How to post on QudosList

From the application’s home screen, tap the top right icon to start sharing on QudosList.

How to post on QudosList

Tap the text box that says Want to share something? You can share updates with your friends and express your appreciation towards a specific Qudos driver or to Qudos’ services.

Tap the Post button. Your added friends and family will get to see your posts by scrolling through the news feed or visiting your profile.

How to share your driver

From the navigation bar, tap the Users icon, then tap QudosFave to display a list of QudosFave drivers. Tap MyFaves to display your own set of QudosFave drivers, and tap the Share button.

Share your thoughts about your ride and put in a good word for friends and family.

How to know more about your Qudos driver

Use the search bar to find the name of your driver. Visit their profile to get to know them, the features they offer, and view ratings.

You can also add them as a QudosFave driver as well as follow and share their profile with other riders.
